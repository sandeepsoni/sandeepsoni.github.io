I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in the Computational Linguistics Lab @ Gatech, advised by Dr. Jacob Eisenstein. My research interests are broadly in computational social science and computational sociolinguistics, and I'm particularly interested in modeling and measuring language change over time and studying its relation to society.
