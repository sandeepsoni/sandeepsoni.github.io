I'm a PhD student in Computer Science working with Jacob Eisenstein at the Georgia Institute of Technology . My broad research interests are in the areas of computational social science and computational sociolinguistics. In my research, I use methods from machine learning, natural language processing, and social networks on problems that involve modeling and measuring language change. Additionally, my research tries to establish the relationship between influence and language change.
